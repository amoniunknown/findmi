FindMI is the leading software solution for Specialists, GPs, and Allied Health professionals, providing real-time savings and effortless management of patient imaging information. Securely access past imaging for new patients or locate imaging for patients who visit multiple providers with ease.

How it works

Empowering healthcare providers.

Welcome to FindMI, the clinical software trusted by thousands of healthcare practitioners across Australia. With FindMI, you can effortlessly manage your team, set permissions, and handle imaging providers and viewer credentials. Our robust patient search feature allows you to quickly locate imaging across all your providers in one intuitive platform.

At FindMI, we are committed to transforming the accessibility and utilisation of medical imaging. Our mission is to empower healthcare providers with a seamless, efficient solution for managing patient imaging data, ultimately enhancing patient care.

Patient-Centric Experience

The FindMI platform is built with a focus on enhancing the overall patient experience. By facilitating easy access to medical imaging, we contribute to a patient-centric approach that prioritises quality care and positive outcomes.

Security

Say goodbye to the hassle of recording, locating or accessing imaging provider viewer credentials. FindMI offers a secure, centralised vault for all clinicians in your practice. We prioritise security, making it a core feature that influences every decision we make.
